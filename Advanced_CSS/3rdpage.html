What is Obsidian Used For?

Obsidian has many uses both in Minecraft and in real life. In Minecraft, it is primarily used to create Nether portals, which allow players to travel between dimensions.

Real-Life Applications

In real life, obsidian has been used historically for making sharp tools, such as arrowheads and knives. Due to its ability to break into extremely sharp edges, it has even been used in modern surgical scalpel blades. Additionally, obsidian is a popular material for decorative objects and jewelry. Volcanic glass, as it is sometimes called, has been highly valued by ancient civilizations for its durability and aesthetic appeal.

Additional Facts

Obsidian forms when lava cools rapidly with minimal crystal growth. It is commonly found near volcanic areas and has a naturally glossy appearance.
